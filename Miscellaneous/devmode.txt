Located your halo.exe SHORTCUT.
Right Click and select PROPERTIES.
You should see this window: https://imgur.com/a/Nz36tjb

In the TARGET field, you should see something like this:
"C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Games\Halo\halo.exe"

Change to this:
"C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Games\Halo\halo.exe" - devmode
Click Apply, then OK.

Open Halo, then press ~
Type "devcam 1" to enable Fly Cam.

Use W.A.S.D to move Middle Mouse button to activate velovity.
